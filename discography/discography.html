IU's

Discography

The South Korean singer-songwriter IU's debut studio album is titled Growing Up. As a follow-up to her 2008 debut mini-album Lost and Found, it was released on April 23, 2009. Boo and "You Know () (Rock Ver.)," two of the 16 songs from the album, were made available as singles.

The second studio album of South Korean singer-songwriter IU is titled Last Fantasy. LOEN Entertainment distributed and published it on November 29, 2011. There are thirteen songs on the album in all, including the lead single "You & I."

"You & I" song was a commercial success in South Korea, topping various music charts and earning IU several awards and nominations. Its unique blend of genres and dreamy atmosphere has made it a beloved classic among IU's fans and a staple of her live performances.

"Palette" is the title track of IU's fourth Korean studio album of the same name, released on April 21, 2017. The song features Korean rapper G-Dragon and is a mellow and introspective pop track. The lyrics reflect IU's thoughts on growing up and finding her own identity as a young adult.

"Lilac" is a retro-inspired pop track with a catchy melody and nostalgic lyrics. The song talks about remembering the good times with someone special and cherishing those memories. The music video features IU in various colorful and retro outfits, dancing and singing in a colorful set.
